I began to select variables as Hugo_Symbol, Variant_Classification, DepMap_ID and isDeleterious. Next I tried to filter out the cell lines that have mutations in RNF43.

I found out that there are two tpyes of frameshift mutations, either deletion or insertion. I combined these two types of frameshift mutations.

Next, I opened the expression dataset and filtered out the expression for gene of interest Axin2.

I joined the two dataset together using the inner_join function.

EDA Graph

I would like to see whether the type of RNF43 mutations will change the AXIN2 expression. I decided to use the boxplot for the graph.

Conclusions

Compared to silent mutations, other types of mutations including frameshift and missense mutations appear to increase the Wnt target gene Axin2 expression in cancer cell lines.

Prioritized follow-up studies

Some cell lines with missense mutations have very high Axin2 levels. It is interesting to see where these mutations are in the protein and whether they are located in a functional domain.
